About Me

I am a rising senior Mechanical Engineering student at Arizona State University, working to fulfill a lifelong dream of working in the space industry. I want to utilize my passions to help humanity explore our solar system and universe. My interests include but are not limited to: rocket propulsion, spacecraft design and manufacturing, test engineering, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, structural analysis, and control systems.

This website is a way for me to showcase what I have learned so far both academically and professionally. Please take a look around and reach out if you have anything you'd like to say!
